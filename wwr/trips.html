Featured Adventures

Los Dientes del Diablo

Expert Level

Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush on this challenging Class IV-V rapid. Los Dientes del Diablo ("The Devil's Teeth") is perfect for experienced paddlers seeking the most intense white water experience. Navigate through powerful waves, sharp turns, and technical sections with our expert guides.

  • Duration: Full Day (8 hours)
  • Distance: 15 miles
  • Group Size: 4-8 people

Big Mallard

Advanced Level

A thrilling journey down one of the region's most scenic and challenging sections of river. Big Mallard offers Class III-IV rapids with breathtaking canyon views. Perfect for adventurers with some rafting experience who want to push their limits while enjoying stunning natural landscapes.

  • Duration: Full Day (7 hours)
  • Distance: 12 miles
  • Group Size: 4-10 people

Half Day Adventure

Intermediate Level

An excellent choice for those seeking excitement without a full day commitment. This Class II-III adventure covers some of the most beautiful sections of the river with exciting rapids and calm pools for recovery. Great for families and groups looking to balance fun with time for other activities.

  • Duration: Half Day (4 hours)
  • Distance: 6 miles
  • Group Size: 4-12 people

Sunset Rapids

Beginner-Friendly

Experience the magic of the river as the sun sets on the horizon. This Class I-II gentle float is perfect for first-time rafters and families with young children. Enjoy peaceful paddling through scenic valleys and calm waters while our guides share knowledge about local wildlife and river ecology.

  • Duration: Evening (3 hours)
  • Distance: 4 miles
  • Group Size: 4-15 people

What's Included?

  • Professional guide and safety briefing
  • All necessary safety equipment
  • Helmet and life jacket
  • High-quality rafting paddle
  • Dry bag for personal items
  • Pre-trip orientation

What to Bring?

  • Swimsuit or water-resistant clothing
  • Water shoes or sandals with straps
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses
  • Light jacket or fleece (for cooler days)
  • Towel and change of clothes
  • Water bottle and snacks

Safety Requirements

  • Minimum age: 8 years (varies by trip)
  • Good physical fitness required
  • Ability to swim recommended
  • Medical clearance if needed
  • Sign liability waiver
  • Follow all guide instructions
